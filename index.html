What is Love?

Love is the force that defies time, the silent whisper in the chaos, the light that refuses to fade. Love knows no walls, no rules, no limits. It is raw, relentless, infinite. Love is everything. Love is everywhere. Love is limitless.

It is the quiet understanding, the mutual confidence, the sharing and forgiving. It is loyalty through good and bad times.
